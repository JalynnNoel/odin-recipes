How To: Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine noodles, and a luscious garlic Parmesan Alfredo sauce.
The velvety texture and savory flavors make it an irresistible comfort food, perfect for a rainy or snowy day inside. Its warmth and indulgence
bring a sense of coziness, making every bite feel like a comforting hug. Paired with garlic bread or a warm drink, itâ€™s the
ultimate meal to enjoy while watching the rain drizzle or snow blanket the world outside.

This recipe makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 16 oz dry fettuccine pasta

    • To Make The Chicken

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp italin seasoning
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 pepper
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter

    • To Make The Sauce

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 clove glaric - minced
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the noodles

    2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook
    untill al denten according to package directions (about 10 min.) Reserve 1/2 cup
    of the cooking water, then drain well and set aside.

  2. Make the chicken

    3. Season chicken breast with the italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Warm the olive oil over
    medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet. Once its shimmering, swirl the pan to evenly distrubte.
    Add the chicken and leave it undisturbed for 5-7 min, until the bottom is golden brown
    Flip over the chicken and add in 1 tbsp of butter, swirl the pan to distribute the butter.
    Continue cooking for another 5-7 min (or until internal temp reaches 165 degrees F). Remove chicken from pan
    and cut into 1/2 inch thick slices and cover.

  3. Make the alfredo sauce

    4. In the same pan over medium-low heat add the butter and cream and whisk until butter is melted
    Add in the minced garlic, garlic powder,italian seasoning, salt and pepper, whisk until combined and smooth
    Bring to a gental simmer and cook for 3-4 min whisking constantly, until it starts to thicken. Stir in the parmesan
    cheese just until melted and the sauce is smooth. If the sauce is took thick ass a few tbsp on the reserved pasta water at time to thin it out.

  4. Assesmble

    5. Take the sauce off the heat and immediately toss with the cooked noodles and in the chicken,
    garnish with parsley, parmesan and black pepperand add garlic bread on the side if desired.

    Enjoy!
