How To: Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine noodles, and a luscious garlic Parmesan Alfredo sauce.
The velvety texture and savory flavors make it an irresistible comfort food, perfect for a rainy or snowy day inside. Its warmth and indulgence
bring a sense of coziness, making every bite feel like a comforting hug. Paired with garlic bread or a warm drink, itâ€™s the
