How To: Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Loaded potato soup is a rich, creamy, and comforting dish packed with tender potatoes, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and savory seasonings.
Often topped with sour cream and green onions, it delivers a hearty, satisfying flavor in every bite. This warm, indulgent soup is the perfect meal
for a cozy rainy or snowy day inside, offering both warmth and nostalgia in a single bowl. Its thick, velvety texture and savory depth make
it an ultimate comfort foodâ€”ideal for curling up with a blanket and watching the rain or snow fall outside.

This recipe makes 8servings

Ingredients

  • 6 strips of uncooked bacon
  • 3 tbsp butter (salted or unsalted)
  • 1 medium yellow onion - chopped
  • 3 larged garlic cloves - minced
  • 2 1/1lbs gold potatoes - peeled and diced (about 6 large potatoes)
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2/3 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ancho chili (add more to taste if needed)
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Shredded cheddar cheese, chives, and additonal sour cream and bacon for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep bacon

    2. Cook bacon pieces until bacon is crisp and brown. Remove bacon and set aside saving the fat

  2. Make a roux

    3. Add butter and chopped onion and cook over medium heat until onions are tender (about 3-5 min).
    Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Next sprinkle the flour over the ingredients in the pot and stir until smooth

  3. Cook the potatoes

    4. Add chicken broth, milk, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and ancho chili powder. Stir well and bring to a boil,
    cook unitl potatos are tender when pierced with a frok (about 10 min)

  4. Final steps

    5. Reduce heat to a simmer and remove about half (or more dependingn on how smooth and creamy you would like your soup)
    to a blender and puree until till smooth. Return the pureed soup back to the pot and add sour cream and bacon pieces set aside earlier
    stir well and allow soup to simmer 15 min before serving.

  5. Toppings

    6. Top with additional sour cream, bacon, cheddar cheese and/or chives to your liking.

    Enjoy!
