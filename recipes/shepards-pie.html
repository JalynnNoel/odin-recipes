How To: Sherpard's Pie

Shepherd’s Pie is a hearty, comforting dish made with seasoned ground meat, vegetables,
and a rich gravy, all topped with creamy mashed potatoes and baked until golden.
Its warm, savory flavors and satisfying texture make it the perfect meal for a rainy or snowy day,
bringing a sense of coziness and home-cooked comfort with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb of Ground Beef (or any meat of your choice)
  • 3 large Russett Potatoes (1 1/2 to 2 lbs)
  • 1-2 cup of frozen mixed vegatable
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Salt & Pepper or other seasonings of choice to taste
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese
  • 1/2 a cup of sour cream

Follow the steps below to make the perfect Shepherd’s Pie

  1. Prepare your vegatables

    2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Then peel and quater the potatoes, place the potatoes into a medium sized pot
    and cover them with at least one inch of cold water and add a teaspoon of salt. Bring to a boil
    While the potatoes are boiling melt 4 tbsp of butter in a large saute pan on medium heat
    add the choped onions and cook for about 6-10 min. or until tender. Add in the frozen vegatables towards the end
    of the cooking time as these take very little to cook.

  2. Add the ground beef

    3. Add the ground beef to the pan with the vegatables and cook until no longer pink.
    Drain the pan of any excess fat and season with salt and pepper, and any other seasonings you would like to add.
    Next add in the Worcestershire sauce and beef broth and bring everything to a simmer and reduce to low heat
    cook uncovered for 10 min adding more beef broth if needed to stop the meat from drying out. Taste everything and add more seasonings if needed.

  3. Make the mash potatoes

    4. When a fork is easily able to pierce through the potate remove them from the pot and place in a bowl with the remeaning 4 tbsp of butter
    Mash with a fork or a potato masher, slowly add in the sour cream until you reach a creamy consitancy of your liking
    and season to taste

  4. Layer everything in a casserole dish

    5. Spread the meat fixture in an even layer in a large baking dish and spread the mashed potatoes over top the meat.
    Finally add as much shredded cheese as you would like on top and place in the oven for about 30 min

    Enjoy!
